Keep architectural control. Build your own framework.


Vike is a set of tools to easily:

  • build your own app architecture, or
  • build your own web framework.

Hundreds of LOCs are enough to build a full-fledged architecture or framework.

Vike tools:

  • vite-plugin-ssr - Like Next.js/Nuxt but as do-one-thing-do-it-well Vite plugin. (Production ready.)
  • Telefunc - Remote Functions. Instead of API. (Production ready.)
  • react-streaming - React 18 Streaming. Full-fledged & Easy. (Production ready.)
  • HatTip - (Nothing) Like Express.js. (WIP.)
  • scaffi - Project Scaffolding. (WIP.)

Vike frameworks:

  • Wind SSG - Static site generator for Vue, like Gridsome but with Vite. (WIP.)
  • Vilay - React 18 + Relay framework with Render-as-you-Fetch SSR streaming. (WIP.)
  • Stem - Like Blitz.js but with Vite and Telefunc. (WIP.)

Keep architectural control

Don't let a framework dictate your stack. With Vike, you keep full control: use any technology and tool you want.

Build your own framework

Build your own framework with only hundreds of LOCs: set up your architecture once, publish it as an npm package, then reap the benefits and re-use your framework for each new project.

All-included frameworks™

While Next.js/Nuxt only takes care of the frontend, you can include all your integrations to your framework: the server (e.g. Express.js), authentication, communication (e.g. GraphQL or RPC), ORM (e.g. Prisma or MikroORM), logging (e.g. Prometheus), error tracking (e.g. Sentry), emailing (e.g. Mailgun + Nodemailer), deploy (e.g. Cloudflare Workers or AWS), etc.

After you've built your all-included framework, all it takes to create a new app is defining data models and UI components.

Scale your team

Give your senior colleagues the power of fully owning the architecture while providing your less-senior colleagues a simple framework that takes care of everything. Use and leverage your company's favorite tools without compromise.

Ecosystem of Frameworks

Vike fosters the creation of frameworks: use an all-included framework built by another company, fork and adjust it to your likings, and share your framework(s) with the world ❤️.

Enhance your product

You are building a headless CMS? An e-commerce platform like Shopify? Delight your users with a tailored framework that deeply integrates with your product.
